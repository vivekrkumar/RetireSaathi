The Problem - The Indian Retirement Dilemma

India is aging rapidly, but most aren't ready for retirement.

  • Low Preparedness: 75% of Indians are skeptical about initiating retirement planning, with many relying on children or basic savings.
  • Complex & Fragmented: The landscape of retirement products (PF, NPS, MFs) is confusing, lacking unified, personalized guidance.
  • Digital Divide in Solutions: While fintech adoption is high, comprehensive digital platforms solely focused on holistic retirement planning for diverse segments are scarce.
  • Untapped Potential: Many formal sector and gig workers lack access to or understanding of robust, diversified retirement strategies.
